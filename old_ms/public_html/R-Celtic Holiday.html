Celtic Holiday

Traditional Christmas Classics on solo Celtic harp

This 2-CD set showcases many of Philip's favorite carols from twenty-six years of performing Celtic Yuletide with his family and friends, presented here in a delightful setting for purely solo harp. Although many of these carols have been performed and recorded with the group in the past, these pieces are all newly recorded and arranged for this intimate and festive musical feast.
