Elegance of Hammered Dulcimer

Traditional favorites, with some fiddle, piano, bass and harmonium

This 2-CD set presents a slightly more eclectic mix of repertoire including some classical favorites, a Hungarian cymbalom tune, and a unique rendition of the popular song “Yellow Bird” from Philip’s Jamaican childhood. Also includes some old-time American tunes reminiscent of Philip’s early “Wild Rose String Band” days, along with a healthy portion of jigs, reels, and haunting airs.
