Musings: Celtic Harp Originals

This sonically lush new CD provides a stunning collection of Philip’s most recent harp compositions, including his much requested slack-key style pieces ‘Ohana Kai and Napili Nights, written on the shores of Maui.  Be taken on a journey to ancient Ireland with the Harp of Cil Riliag, and fall asleep to beautiful lullabies composed for the newest granddaughters and grand nieces. Most innovative and heartfelt are the lyrical ‘planxties’ written in honor of notable friends – all recorded this November at the world-class Big Red Studio, engineered and co-produced by Billy Oskay.
