Whispers of the Muse – Sonnets from Magic Hill

Philip’s long-awaited first book of poetry, written over a period of 17 years, contains a collection of sonnets and verse, both whimsical and poignant, chronicling his musical journey and family life.  Taking after his father, Kenneth Boulding, a world-famous economist and humanitarian who wrote hundreds of sonnets during his lifetime, Whispers of the Muse strikes a universal chord in the midst of our common human journey.
