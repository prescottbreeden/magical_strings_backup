February 12, 2004

Dear Fellow Harpers and Students—

Classes are starting up next week for the School of Magical Strings, (Seattle Tuesday, Feb. 17, Bellevue Wednesday, Feb. 18) and I want to encourage those of you who would like to take the beginning or intermediate class, but are facing obstacles to doing so… remember that in spite of hard times, music and art are as important to the soul as food and shelter are to the body! And the harp heals all wounds…

I would like to invite ALL those interested in the harp at any level to attend the first class in Seattle or Bellevue: the beginners, for a full-rounded introduction to the harp; and for returning students, a chance to get your harp fine-tuned, do some warm-up exercises and review. No charge and no commitment to continue with the class - and depending on the turnout, I may add another week to the course. As a further enticement to prospective intermediate students, we have recently returned from doing workshops and concerts on the ‘warm island’ of Maui, and have some exciting new techniques relating to the gorgeous music of the traditional Hawaiian ‘slack key’ guitar style, tailor-made for the Celtic harp!

If you think you might come, or have any questions, I'd appreciate hearing from you, but not required - just come! You can CLICK HERE to send me a note.

Cheers and Aloha,

Philip Boulding
