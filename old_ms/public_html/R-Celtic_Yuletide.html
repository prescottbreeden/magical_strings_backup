CELTIC YULETIDE – Celebrating 31 Years Live!

Pick up a copy of our new CD, featuring stunning performances from last year’s concerts and including several of our newest compositions, The Fairy Wind, Barbara’s Cottage, and Dance of the Wind Spirits. Bring the irresistible and uplifting spirit of Celtic Yuletide into your home this season, and sing along with the best in the Northwest!  Three generations come together in this brilliant festival of Irish and holiday music, dance and storytelling to bring joy and warmth into the heart of winter.
