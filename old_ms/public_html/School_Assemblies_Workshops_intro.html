With dancing hammers and pulsating strings, Philip and Pam Boulding have created the music of Magical Strings, described by the Washington Post as “…warm, graceful and sonically gorgeous.” Celtic harps and hammered dulcimers are augmented by penny whistles, concertina, valiha (Malagasy bamboo harp), and storytelling, with captivating arrangements intertwining Celtic roots, classical and world music influences.
